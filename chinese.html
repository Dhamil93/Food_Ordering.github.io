CHINESE 中国菜

Chinese food is possibly the most popular cuisine in the world. So much so, Just Eat revealed in 2018 that Ireland's heavy hitter takeaway choice with the highest number of orders in the year was in fact Chinese takeout. This makes it an Irish favorite, alongside American and 'the traditional chipper'.
Let's cover a few spots in Cork where you can get your hands on some.
